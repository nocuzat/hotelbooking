Welcome to LoungeHotel

Since 2005 LoungeHotel has built a fine reputation for offering luxury carefully situated in the mainstream of regional business and travel destinations in South Africa.

LoungeHotel was established when current Managing Director Thulani Nocuza purchased an old hotel in Centurion in Gauteng, with a view to turning it into a boutique establishment. Offering discerning clients a home away from home, the first branded LoungeHotel was born.
